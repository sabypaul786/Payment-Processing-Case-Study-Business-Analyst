# Problem Summary

The payment ecosystem was established but had inefficiencies due to fragmentation across multiple checkout systems.

### 🔴 Key Issues

* Multiple systems handling payments independently
* No standardized data structure
* Poor payment failure handling
* Orders getting blocked due to failed payments
* No intelligent retry mechanism
* Reconciliation challenges due to data mismatch
* Lack of audit trail
* High dependency on manual processes

---

### 💥 Business Impact

* Revenue loss due to failed transactions
* Poor customer experience
* Seller dissatisfaction
* Increased operational cost
* Slow scalability and feature rollout

---

### 🎯 Core Problem

The issue was not absence of a system, but lack of:

* Consistency
* Visibility
* Efficiency
